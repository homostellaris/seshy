    Welcome to Seshy. The page you were just on is called the session manager. It contains lists of session cards.

    At first sessions are unsaved bookmark_border.

    Clicking the edit edit button on a session card allows you to give it a name.

    Naming a session makes it a saved session bookmark.

    Saved sessions are stored as bookmarks in a folder under Other Bookmarks/Seshy so they are synced with your other devices.

    Saved sessions become shelved sessions when their window is closed which means they can be resumed at any time by selecting their session card and pressing ENTER.

    Ensure that you close a saved session by closing the window rather than closing all the tabs. Seshy re-saves sessions after every change to a tab so closing all the tabs results in a saved session with only one tab in it!
